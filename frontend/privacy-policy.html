Privacy Policy

iCrown Dental Clinic Management System

Effective Date: January 31, 2026
Last Updated: January 31, 2026

1. Introduction

iCrown Dental Clinic ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting the privacy and security of personal data in accordance with the Data Privacy Act of 2012 (Republic Act No. 10173) of the Philippines and other applicable data protection laws.

This Privacy Policy governs our collection, use, storage, and protection of personal data obtained through our dental clinic management system.

2. Definitions

  • Personal Data: Any information, whether recorded in material form or not, from which the identity of an individual is apparent or can be reasonably and directly ascertained.
  • Sensitive Personal Information: Personal data about an individual's race, ethnic origin, marital status, age, color, and religious, philosophical or political affiliations.
  • Data Subject: An individual whose personal data is processed.
  • Processing: Any operation or set of operations performed upon personal data.

3. Data Collection

We collect the following types of personal data:

Patient Information:
  • Full name, date of birth, age, gender
  • Contact information (address, phone number, email)
  • Medical and dental history
  • Treatment records and procedures
  • Insurance information
  • Payment and billing information
System Usage Data:
  • Login credentials and access logs
  • System usage patterns
  • Technical information about devices used

4. Legal Basis for Processing

We process personal data based on the following legal grounds under the Data Privacy Act:

  • Consent: When you explicitly provide consent for specific processing activities
  • Contract: When processing is necessary for our dental service contract
  • Legal Obligation: When required by Philippine laws and regulations
  • Legitimate Interest: For legitimate business interests in dental healthcare

5. Data Usage

We use your personal data for the following purposes:

  • Providing dental healthcare services
  • Managing appointments and schedules
  • Maintaining patient records and medical history
  • Billing and payment processing
  • Communication with patients
  • Compliance with legal and regulatory requirements
  • Quality improvement and research (with consent)

6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your personal data only under the following circumstances:

  • With your explicit consent
  • With healthcare providers involved in your treatment
  • With insurance companies for billing purposes
  • With government authorities when required by law
  • With service providers who assist in our operations

All data sharing is done in accordance with the Data Privacy Act and relevant healthcare regulations.

7. Data Security

We implement appropriate organizational, physical, and technical security measures to protect your personal data, including:

  • Encryption of sensitive data
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Regular security audits
  • Employee training on data protection
  • Secure data storage and transmission

8. Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes for which it was collected, in accordance with:

  • Philippine Medical Records retention requirements
  • Tax and legal document retention periods
  • Healthcare regulatory requirements

Medical records are typically retained for at least 5 years from the last patient contact, as required by Philippine regulations.

9. Your Data Privacy Rights

As a data subject, you have the following rights under the Data Privacy Act:

  • Right to be Informed: Know what personal data is being collected
  • Right to Access: Request access to your personal data
  • Right to Correct: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of data when no longer necessary
  • Right to Object: Object to processing of your data
  • Right to Data Portability: Request transfer of your data
  • Right to File a Complaint: Report privacy violations to the National Privacy Commission

10. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 18 without parental consent. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children without parental consent.

11. International Data Transfers

Your personal data is stored and processed within the Philippines. Any international data transfers will comply with the Data Privacy Act and relevant regulations.

12. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

13. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated through our system or other appropriate means.
